# Furman University CEX Files

A repository of digital libraries in CEX format, for quotidian teaching and research at Furman University.

## Files

All texts have licensing information present in the CEX file.

- `iliad-1.cex` Three texts of *Iliad* Book 1: An edition, based on that of Allen, from the Open Greek and Latin project, and two tokenized exemplars, one of word-tokens, and one of lemmatized tokens, created by Giuseppe Celano. <http://www.dh.uni-leipzig.de/wo/projects/open-greek-and-latin-project/>.
- `fu-spring2018.cex` Contents include the contents of `iliad-1.cex`, above, and Herodotus' *Histories* in Greek and English, courtesy of the Open Greek and Latin project.
